Action recommendations

Open Conversation about Morals:

Initiate a relaxed and non-judgmental conversation about moral perspectives. Sharing stories from your past or hypothetical scenarios can make this easier. Remember, the goal is to understand, not to convince or win.

Small Gestures of Physical Affection:

Given the nature of your relationship and it being not measured in defined time, you can build on physical closeness gradually. Start with small, non-threatening gestures like holding hands, or a hug after a long day.

Introduce an Adventure

Given the fact you've been dating for 11 years with no children involved, consider planning a unique adventure or trip. It could be a trek, a road trip or even exploring a new hobby together. Shared experiences can build bonds in unexpected ways and might create a comfort space for increased physical closeness.
